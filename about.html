BIO

About me

Hi, I'm Sandy. I am an artist living in Spain. I am a fan of photography, design and the arts. I am also interested in movies, video games and painting. I am 22 years old.

I have always liked to draw and I think there are few things in the world that I like more than that, in fact, that passion for art spread to me studying art at the Sagrada Familia in Moratalaz, later, I discovered the fantastic world of 3D and all its branches and I decided to study a higher degree in 3D animation, video games and interactive environment design at U-tad, where I discovered my true vocation which is to be a character designer and modeler and thus I finished my training at Lightbox Academy doing a master in organic modeling and another in concept art.
